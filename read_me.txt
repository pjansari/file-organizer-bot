File Organizer.

Organizes files based on the extension to folders named:

"Images": [".jpg", ".jpeg", ".png", ".gif"],
"Documents": [".pdf", ".docx", ".txt", ".csv"],
"Videos": [".mp4", ".avi", ".mkv"],
"Music": [".mp3", ".wav"],
"Archives": [".zip", ".rar", ".tar"],
"Executables: [".exe", ".sh", ".bat"]

add more categories, or extensions, based on your need, to the script.
